Spacing

Based on an 8px grid. All spacing values are multiples of the base unit.

4px XS
8px SM
16px MD
24px LG
32px XL
48px XXL

Usage guide:

XS (4px) -- tight inline spacing, icon gaps
SM (8px) -- element padding, compact lists
MD (16px) -- card padding, form field gaps
LG (24px) -- section padding, card grid gaps
XL (32px) -- section margins
XXL (48px) -- page padding, major section breaks

Kitchen buttons (80px): Cooks may have wet or floured hands. Extra-large targets reduce mistakes under pressure.
